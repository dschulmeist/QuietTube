# Privacy Policy — QuietTube

**Last updated:** March 18, 2026

## Summary

QuietTube does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Period.

## Data Collection

QuietTube collects **no data whatsoever**. Specifically:

- No personal information
- No browsing history
- No usage analytics or telemetry
- No cookies or tracking pixels
- No financial or payment information
- No authentication credentials

## Data Storage

The only data QuietTube stores is your feature toggle preferences (e.g., whether "Hide Shorts" is on or off). These settings are saved locally in your browser using `chrome.storage.sync`, which is a built-in Chrome API that syncs settings across your signed-in Chrome devices. This data never leaves your browser and is not accessible to anyone — including us.

## Third-Party Services

QuietTube makes **no network requests**. It does not communicate with any external servers, APIs, or third-party services.

## Permissions

QuietTube requests the following browser permissions:

| Permission | Why |
|---|---|
| `storage` | To save your toggle preferences locally |
| `activeTab` | To apply settings to the current YouTube tab |
| `*://*.youtube.com/*` | To inject CSS and scripts that hide distracting elements on YouTube |

These permissions are the minimum required for the extension to function. No other sites are accessed.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the update will be reflected here with a new "Last updated" date. Given that QuietTube collects no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.
